# 2021

I was getting a bit worn out towards the end of the month, and when I saw day 19 it reminded me of the sea monsters problem from last year.
Remembering how long that one took me to solve, I ignored that day. Some other tough problems towards the end also remain unsolved.

During December, I got 43 stars, missing Day 19, 24, 25 and 22 part 2.

Update: I came back to this in 2026 and solved the remaining puzzles.
I got good use of some of the best advice Tomas has caught me,
"wishful thinking" and "divide and conquer".
When tackling a challenging problem, just wish that you had the helper functions you need,
i.e. "I could solve this hard problem if I just had functions X, Y, Z".
Now you have reduced the problem to implementing some simpler functions.
Rinse and repeat until you get to the leaf functions and have your solution.
As a bonus, using this method the solutions tend to be clean instead of just one big, messy function.
With this method, I was able to "one-shot" (i.e. the solution worked the first time I tried it) day 19 and day 22 part 2,
which I had previously not dared to even touch.
